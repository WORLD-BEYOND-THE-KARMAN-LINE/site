About Us

World Beyond the Karman Line is a non-profit space magazine led by a team of space enthusiasts, educators, and science writers, dedicated to inspiring curiosity and expanding horizons beyond Earth’s edge.

We exist purely to share knowledge, spark imagination, and celebrate the wonders of space science and exploration and hence do not generate any revenue or operate commercially—our mission is driven solely by passion, learning, and the spirit of discovery.

Join us as we explore the wonders of the universe, one issue at a time!
